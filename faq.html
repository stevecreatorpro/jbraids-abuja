Common Questions

Everything you need to know before your appointment.

Is the "Pain-Free Guarantee" real?

Yes! This is our strict policy. We do not grip your edges tightly, and our knotless method ensures zero tension on your scalp. You should be able to sleep comfortably on the very same day.

Do I need to wash my hair before I come?

We prefer you arrive with detangled, washed, and blown-out hair. However, if you'd like us to handle that, simply add the "Wash & Deep Condition" add-on when booking for just ₦5,000.

Do you provide the extensions/attachments?

Our prices cover the workmanship only. We kindly ask clients to bring their preferred colour and brand of extensions to ensure complete satisfaction. We will text you exactly how many packs to bring after you book.

How long do Knotless Braids typically last?

With proper care, wrapping at night, and moisturizing your scalp, small to medium knotless braids can last 6 to 8 weeks beautifully.

Cancellation & Late Policy

We operate strictly on appointments to ensure no one waits. Please be respectful of our stylists' time.

  • Lateness: A grace period of 20 minutes is given. After 30 minutes, your appointment may be canceled or shortened.
  • Cancellation: Please cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours in advance using the link in your confirmation SMS.
  • No-Shows: Clients who no-show without notice will be required to pay a 50% non-refundable deposit for future bookings.
